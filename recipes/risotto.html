RISOTTO MEAT

That`s a simple dish, perfect suited for people who do not undertsand a thing a about cook. Because the recipe use simple itens, who everbody has, and didn`t need complicated steps.

INGREDIENTS
  • 1 cup of rice
  • half onion
  • 2 tomatoes
  • half of bell pepper
  • 100ml of olive oil
  • 500g of Meat
  • 200ml of bordo wine
  • 3 head of garlic
  • 200 ml of milk cream
PREPARATION
  1. First you need grease the pan with the olive oil
  2. Saute half potion of garlic, onions, tomatoes and bell pepper
  3. When the greens starts to be soft you put the rice and put 3 cups of water, and keep replenish of water until the rice is cooked
  4. When the rice is cooking you pick another pan, and fried the MEAT with the other half potion of greens
  5. When the rice is about to be cooked you add the meat with the rest of greens and put the 200ml of bordo wine, and 200ml of milk cream, then close. Everething will be ready at 10 min of fire.
NOTE:

That`s will give 5 potions
